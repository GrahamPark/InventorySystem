1.) in the GUISkin folder, rightclick and click create-GUI skin, and name it.

2.) click the newly created GUI skin.

3.) click the button tab in the inspector.

4.) place created textures for the idle button(Normal), and hover(Hover)

5.) click on window, and place your window texture for normal, and on normal.

6.) drag and drop the newly created GUI Skin onto the backpack skin slot in the inspector for the inventory.

7.) place your textures for the following locations in the inventory system inspector:
	
	inventory - selected Texture
	inventory - highlighted Texture
	inventory - backpack - backpack_Backdrop
	inventory - hotbar - hotbar_Textures(elements 0-8)
	armour - armour Textures(elements 0-2)
	stats - images - health bar
	stats - images - thirst bar
	stats - images - hunger bar
	stats - images - stat Back Drop
